Thrilling Performance

Powering the 2022 Jaguar XF is the same turbocharged 2.0-Litre four-cylinder engine, which comes in two states of tune. The P50 version of the XF produces 246 HP, whereas the P300 makes around 296 HP. The base-level engine is fairly solid, but for those looking for an extra punch, the P300 is a fabulous choice.

Competitively Priced

The XF overflows with English luxury charm, even if it isn't as well-rounded as some of its competitors. Its simple, elegant sheet metal and sporty stance fit a Jaguar. With the latest updates, the cabin is better than ever.

Fine British Interior

The interior of the XF is finely built and features loads of soft-touch materials and leather. The vehicle has ample knee room, headroom, and shoulder room for a vehicle in its class. It has a wheelbase of 116.5 inches while allowing the XF to seat 5 in comfort

Unique Luxury Performance

It has sophisticated Jaguar-like styling, packs in good performance numbers, and is convenient. The car is inspired by the F-type sports car, and for 2022, the F-Pace has received a minor facelift over the previous generation on the outside and the inside
